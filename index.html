Dhaqan Plus

Memory before borders. Culture before noise.

Dhaqan Plus is a cultural project exploring Somali history, identity, and collective memory through short, reflective writing.

The project documents what was lived — land, paths, poetry, language — and shares it in a calm, timeless tone for modern audiences.

Content is published automatically and alternates between English and Somali, promoting unity, fairness, and shared heritage beyond divisions.

Dhaqan Plus is an experiment in how technology can support cultural continuity and thoughtful public discourse.
