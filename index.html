Dhaqan Plus

Memory before borders · Culture before noise

Dhaqan Plus is a cultural project documenting Somali history, identity, and collective memory through calm, reflective writing.

Before borders were drawn, meaning was carried — in land, in language, in poetry, and in shared responsibility to one another.

Somali history was carried, not written.
Memory was our archive.
Poetry our law.
Identity arrived early.

This project is published through automation, alternating between English and Somali, and designed to encourage unity, fairness, and continuity across generations.

Dhaqan Plus explores how technology can serve culture — quietly, responsibly, and with dignity.
